Welcome to the Forence Science Club


The Forensic Science Club was founded in 2019. Goals of the the Forensic Science Club is to promote camaraderie amongst students, professors, and professionals in the Forensic Science community. The club provides information about scholarships, internships, job assistance, networking oppurtunities and to promote student awareness and the use of technology in Forensic Science.
